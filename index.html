Privacy Policy for Translate & Play

Last updated: October 2025

Translate & Play (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile game.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following data:

  • Your selected language and game progress
  • Anonymous device and usage data
  • Purchase data through Google Play Billing (handled by Google)
  • Advertising identifiers (for AdMob personalization, if enabled)
  • Analytics data via Google Analytics and Clicky
  • Account information (if you sign in through Supabase)

2. How We Use Your Data

We use this information to:

  • Store your game progress and preferences
  • Provide and manage in-app purchases (lives, hints, crystals)
  • Display ads and reward users for watching ads
  • Analyze usage and improve gameplay experience

3. Advertising and Analytics

We use third-party services to deliver ads and collect analytics:

  • Google AdMob – displays ads and may use your device’s advertising ID for ad personalization and frequency capping. Learn more: Google Ads Policy
  • Google Analytics – helps us understand user behavior and improve app performance. Learn more: Google Privacy Policy
  • Clicky Analytics – collects anonymized traffic data to monitor usage. Learn more: Clicky Privacy Policy

You can opt out of personalized ads by adjusting your device’s ad settings or visiting https://adssettings.google.com.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We and our partners may use cookies or similar tracking technologies to analyze trends, administer the app, and deliver relevant ads. These technologies collect information such as app usage and device type, but not personally identifiable information.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Limited data may be shared with:

  • Google Play – for processing purchases and billing
  • Google AdMob – for displaying ads
  • Google Analytics & Clicky – for app usage statistics
  • Supabase – for authentication and storing progress

6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You can request deletion of your data by contacting us at translateplayapp@gmail.com.

8. Compliance with Privacy Laws

GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation):
If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, or object to its processing. Contact: translateplayapp@gmail.com

CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act):
If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what data we collect, request its deletion, or opt out of data sale (we do not sell data). Contact: translateplayapp@gmail.com

9. Children’s Privacy

Translate & Play is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. All updates will be posted on this page with the revised date.

11. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
